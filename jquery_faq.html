Acadia

  • The Park was Named After a Region of Greece
  • Acadia Was Founded by Private Citizens
  • The Park Is Home to More Than 1,000 Plant Species
  • It is in Maine

Biscayne

  • 95% of the park’s nearly 173,000 acres are underwater. Other than the Convoy Point area near the visitor center, a boat is needed to access the park.
  • The largest island in the park is Elliott Key, which is actually the first of the “true” Florida Keys.
  • Biscayne Bay is generally a very shallow sea, with an average depth of only 10 feet.
  • It is in Flordia

Carlsbad Caverns

  • Carlsbad Cavern is one of over 300 limestone caves in a fossil reef laid down by an inland sea about 265 million years ago
  • Lechuguilla Cave is the deepest limestone cave in the United States. It is the fifth-longest cave on the planet.
  • The bats here are Mexican free-tail bats and as many as 300,000 of them billow out of the caverns each night.
  • It is in New Mexico
